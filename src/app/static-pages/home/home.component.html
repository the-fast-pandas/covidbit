The coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic has changed Canadian lives forever, from how we live to how we work, and how we interact with others. In particular, it has negatively impacted small businesses. Our goal is to help small businesses in the GTA maneuver this new world and allow users to make well-informed decisions.
